Help Center

Find answers to common questions or contact us for further assistance.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in your details, and submit the form. You’ll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How can I earn rewards?

You can earn rewards by completing surveys, tasks, or offers listed on your dashboard. Make sure to follow the instructions provided for each task.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Once you reach the minimum payout threshold, go to the "Withdraw" section in your account, select your preferred payout method, and submit a request. Payments are processed within 3–5 business days.

What if I have issues with my account?

If you experience issues with your account, such as login problems or account suspension, contact our support team using the "Contact Us" page or email us at support@earnwithus.com.

Can I use multiple accounts?

No, each user is allowed only one account. Multiple accounts may result in suspension and forfeiture of earnings.
